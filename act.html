Beach Volleyball

All day long! Jump around in the sand while having fun slapping balls back and forth across the net. Our vast beach provides enough room for many sand courts. Knock yourself out! This activity lasts the all day.

Jet Skiing

Jet free-style around Jamaica Bay, wind through your hair and sea spray on your face in the warm sunrays.

Swimming

Swimming with Whale Sharks in their natural habitat! Although massive, Whale Sharks are docile and harmless, happily doing their own thing as snorkels float around them. To be in their presence is a privilege and an experience you will never forget.

Deck and Bar

After fun in the water, relax with great food and drink on the beautiful deck at Moringa Bay where authentic and tasty cuisine from all arount the world, friendly staff and good music are in abundance.

Scuba diving

Discover the hidden treasures stuck at the bottom of the sea. The waters are calm and clear - perfect for the beginner as well as the advanced diver.
